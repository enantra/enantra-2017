Varun Agarwal

×

Varun Agarwal is a first generation entrepreneur, film maker and author. Still in his twenties, he has three companies to his credit. Co founder of AlmaMater- India's largest company for student merchandise, Varun Agarwal has been featured on the cover of India Today. He is the epitome of progress and a true student hero. His success has inspired thousands of student entrepreneurs.

Dr. Velumani

×

Dr. Velumani is an Indian scientist turned entrepreneur. His success story is one that people look up to in awe. From 3 lakhs to 3300 crores and 19 years of experience in the diagnostic business, his reputation as both scientist and entrepreneur cannot be overshadowed. With multiple degrees and extensive field knowledge, he is the founder, acting chairman and managing director of Thyrocare technologies.

Co founder, Sujayath Ali is a dexterous software development engineer and an internet entrepreneur. He is the acting CEO of Voonik and has innate qualities that enable him to sustain and develop a franchise in such an aggressive market.
